Japan is an island country in East Asia, famous for its unique culture, technology, traditions, and beautiful nature. Popular places:

  • Tokyo — the modern capital with skyscrapers and neon lights.
  • Kyoto — city of ancient temples and gardens.
  • Osaka — known for street food and its castle.
  • Mount Fuji — the tallest and most iconic mountain.
  • Hiroshima — city of peace and history.
  • Okinawa — tropical beaches and island nature.
