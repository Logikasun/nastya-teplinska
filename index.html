Anime - is a Japanese style of animation known for its colorful art, unique characters, and exciting stories that often explore fantasy, adventure, romance, and deep emotions.

Here are some cool facts about anime

  • The first anime appeared in 1917.
  • There are genres for everyone: comedy, romance, horror, sci-fi, and more.
  • Many anime are based on manga (Japanese comics).
  • Japan has anime museums and cosplay festivals where fans dress up as their favorite characters.
